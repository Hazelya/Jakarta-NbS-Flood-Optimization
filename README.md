# Optimizing Nature-Based Solutions for Flood Management in Jakarta : A Multi-Criteria and Spatial Approach

The main objective of this study is to evaluate and compare the effectiveness of several types of Nature-based  Solutions (NbS) to reduce flooding in Jakarta: green roofs, urban forests, wetlands, permeable pavements, urban  parks, retention basins…
